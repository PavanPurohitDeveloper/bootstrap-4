container - grouping all your websites main content. we can have more than one container but we use one container.

xs - extra small screen  -  col-xs-12 
sm - small screen        -  col-sm-6 
md - medium screen       -  col-md-4
lg - large screen        -  col-lg-3
xl - extra large screen  -  col-xl-2

-> Maximum of 12 grd spaces in a single row.

offsetting columns - to move the elements/columns to the right.

- column ordering:
    push - will move to the right
    pull - will move to the left
